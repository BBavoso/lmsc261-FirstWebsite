About Unity

Unity is one of the most popular game engines in the world, known for its versatility and user-friendly interface. It uses C# as its primary programming language and supports development for over 25 different platforms, including PC, consoles, mobile devices, and VR/AR. Unity is particularly favored by indie developers and studios for its accessibility and powerful features, making it an excellent choice for both 2D and 3D game development.
